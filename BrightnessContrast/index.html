Brightness/contrast¶

A dialogue box with two sliders appears with one option for brightness and a second option for contrast. The Brightness control is used to make an image lighter or darker. * Drag slider to the right to increase brightness * Drag slider to the left to decrease brightness

The Contrast control is used or to make an image more vivid or dull, itâ€™s the difference between dark colors and light colors. Low contrast images have very muted tones or colors, whereas high contrast images have vibrant tones. * Drag slider to the right to increase contrast * Drag slider to the left to decrease contrast
